Ritualistic Auditory & Visual Project

sonic dread / queer rites
Blood and Dust is a collaboration of two dark queerdos inspired by horror cinema and occultism. Part horror drone, part spookfolk, part post-rock, they blend synth and cello into nightmarish auditory rituals.
It is the music you hear while wandering a cursed manor at night, calling you to the basement where dreadful things happen. Bring your salt, keep your lantern lit, and stay safe while communing with the dead.
